Sunday Prep Guide

90 minutes on Sunday = effortless meals all week.

Prep Order
1

Cook rice (20 min, hands-off)

Rinse 400 g rice, cook 2:1 water ratio. Cover and leave on low. Spread on a tray to cool completely — never store hot rice.

2

Roast potatoes & sweet potatoes (30 min, hands-off)

Cube 1 kg potatoes + 500 g sweet potato. Toss with olive oil, salt, paprika. Roast at 200 °C on two trays. Check rice while this cooks.

3

Cook chicken breast (20 min)

Season 4–5 chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, garlic, herbs. Pan-fry or bake until cooked through. Let rest, then slice. Store cold.

4

Boil eggs (10 min)

Boil 6 eggs for 8 minutes. Cool in cold water, peel, and store in the fridge — or keep unpeeled up to 1 week.

5

Prep fresh veg (10 min)

Slice cucumber, halve cherry tomatoes, dice red onion. Store each separately in small containers or zip bags.

6

Make 2 dressings (5 min)

Lemon-olive oil: 3 tbsp oil + lemon juice + salt + mustard. Tahini: 2 tbsp tahini + lemon + water + garlic. Store in sealed jars.

7

Store everything correctly

Pack cooled rice and potatoes in airtight containers. Label with date. Chicken separate. Everything lasts 4 days in the fridge.

Storage Guide
🍗

Cooked Chicken

Airtight container, sliced. Mid-fridge shelf, separate from other items.

Up to 4 days
🍚

Cooked Rice

Cool completely first. Airtight container. Freeze half for week 2 in portions.

4 days fridge · 2 months frozen
🥔

Roasted Potatoes

Cool first, then airtight. Don't freeze — texture suffers. Reheat in a dry pan or eat cold.

Up to 4 days
🥚

Boiled Eggs

Keep unpeeled in the fridge for up to 1 week. Peel just before eating.

Up to 7 days unpeeled
🥒

Cut Vegetables

Each veg in its own container. Tomatoes and cucumber stay crispest stored dry.

Up to 4 days
🫙

Dressings

Glass jars with lids. Tahini dressing may thicken — add a splash of water and shake.

Up to 5 days
