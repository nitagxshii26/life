Rainbow Veggie Bowl

A nutrient-dense masterpiece featuring crunchy raw vegetables and roasted roots atop a protein-rich grain base.

⏱
Prep Time
15 min
🍽
Cook Time
20 min
⚡
Calories
420 kcal
👥
Servings
2 Portions

🛒 Ingredients

1 cup Quinoa, cooked
1 cup Purple Cabbage, shredded
2 Carrots, julienned
1 Red Bell Pepper, sliced
½ cup Edamame, steamed
1 Sweet Potato, cubed and roasted
Tahini Dressing (2 tbsp)

📋 Instructions

1
Prepare the Grain Base
Rinse quinoa under cold water. Combine 1 cup quinoa with 2 cups water in a small pot. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 15 minutes until fluffy.
2
Roast the Sweet Potatoes
Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized at the edges.
3
Chop and Steam
While roasting, julienne the carrots and shred the cabbage. Briefly steam the edamame for 3–5 minutes until bright green and tender.
4
Assemble and Dress
Divide the cooked quinoa into two bowls. Arrange the vegetables in colorful sections around the bowl. Drizzle generously with creamy tahini dressing before serving.
