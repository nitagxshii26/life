Sunday prep
guide.

90 minutes on Sunday = effortless meals all week. Here's the exact order to do everything.

Sunday Prep Order
1

Cook rice (20 min, hands-off)

Rinse 400g rice, cook in pot (2:1 water ratio). Cover and leave on low. Spread on a tray to cool completely before storing.

2

Roast potatoes & sweet potatoes (30 min, hands-off)

Cube 1kg potatoes + 500g sweet potato. Toss with olive oil, salt, paprika. Roast at 200°C on two trays. Check rice while this cooks.

3

Cook chicken breast (20 min)

Season 4–5 chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, garlic, herbs. Pan-fry or bake until cooked through. Let rest, then slice into strips. Store cold.

4

Boil eggs (10 min)

Boil 6 eggs for 8 minutes for hard-boiled. Cool in ice water, peel, and store in the fridge (or keep unpeeled up to 1 week).

5

Prep fresh veg (10 min)

Slice cucumber, halve cherry tomatoes, dice red onion. Store each separately in small containers or zip bags.

6

Make 2 dressings (5 min)

Lemon-olive oil: 3 tbsp olive oil + juice of 1 lemon + salt + mustard. Tahini: 2 tbsp tahini + lemon juice + water + garlic. Store in jars.

7

Store everything correctly

Pack cooled rice and potatoes into airtight containers. Label with date. Chicken in a separate airtight container. Everything lasts 4 days in the fridge.

Storage Guide
🍗

Cooked Chicken

Airtight container, sliced and ready. Keep on a mid-fridge shelf. Separate from other items.

Up to 4 days
🍚

Cooked Rice

Cool completely first — never store hot rice. Airtight container. For week 2, freeze half in portions.

4 days fridge · 2 months frozen
🥔

Roasted Potatoes

Cool first, then airtight container. Don't freeze — texture suffers. Reheat in a dry pan or eat cold.

Up to 4 days
🥚

Boiled Eggs

Keep unpeeled in the fridge for up to 1 week. Peel just before eating to keep them fresh.

Up to 7 days unpeeled
🥒

Cut Vegetables

Each veg in its own small container or bag. Tomatoes and cucumber stay crispest when stored dry.

Up to 4 days
🫙

Dressings

Glass jars with lids. Tahini dressing may thicken — add a splash of water and shake before using.

Up to 5 days
Packing Your Work Bowl
☀️

Morning of (5 minutes)

Grab your base (rice or potato), add protein (chicken or eggs), pile on the fresh veg, drizzle your dressing over last. Seal. The bowls taste great cold — no microwave needed.

🧊

At work

Refrigerate until 3–4pm. Eat directly from the container. No heating required — these bowls are designed to be eaten at room temperature, just like the restaurant versions you loved.
